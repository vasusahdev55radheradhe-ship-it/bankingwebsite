Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my password? ‚ñº

You can change your password from the Profile & Settings page. Go to the Security tab and click on "Change Password". Enter your current password and your new password twice, then click Update.

What is the transfer limit? ‚ñº

The default daily transfer limit is $10,000. You can modify this limit from your Profile & Settings. International transfers may have different limits based on regulatory requirements.

How do I download my bank statements? ‚ñº

You can download statements from the Accounts page. Click on an account and select "Download Statement". Choose the date range and format (PDF or Excel), then click download.

Is my account secure? ‚ñº

Yes, your account is protected with advanced encryption and security measures. We recommend enabling two-factor authentication for additional security. You can do this from the Profile & Settings page.

How long does an international transfer take? ‚ñº

International transfers typically take 2-5 business days depending on the destination country and intermediary banks. SWIFT transfers are usually faster than traditional correspondent banking.
